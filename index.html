Hello! I am a postdoc researcher at Microsoft Research, India where I work on the CARE (Clinically Aligned Radiology Expertise) project with Mercy Ranjit.

My research interests lie in medical image analysis, with a focus on self-supervised learning, multimodal representation learning, discrete representations, and grounding clinical concepts into learned models. I work on developing interpretable and robust methods for diverse medical data modalities—including 2D imaging, 3D imaging, radiology reports, and physiological signals. Currently, I am contributing to the CARE (Clinically Aligned Radiology Expertise) project at Microsoft Research India, working towards clinically aligned radiology AI systems.
